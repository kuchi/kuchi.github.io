Novel Multi-Point Scan Architecture Patent Application

Anthony Tanbakuchi

2012-05-10 19:34

Source

This is a patent application that arose from my dissertation and journal publication where I was able to demonstrate through monte-carlo modeling that a slit scan system could be improved by making a small modification so that it becomes a multi-point scan system.

Citation: A. Gimitro, A. Rouse, A. Tanbakuchi, "Novel Multi-Point Scan Architecture," Application number US13266165, 2012. PDF.
