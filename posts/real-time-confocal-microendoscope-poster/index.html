Real-Time Confocal Microendoscope Poster

Anthony Tanbakuchi

2008-01-01 20:01

Source

While at the University of Arizona, my colleagues and I were developing this instrument in the Biomedical Imaging Laboratory, Department of Radiology. The long term goal of this instrument is to replace biopsies with live video images of the cells so surgeons can precisely isolate cancerous tumors for resection. You can read more about it in my dissertation.
